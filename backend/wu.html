CAD vs INR information

CAD – Canadian Dollar

The Canadian Dollar is the official currency of Canada. The currency code for the Canadian Dollar is CAD.

INR – Indian Rupee

The Indian Rupee is the official currency of India. The currency code for the Indian Rupee is INR.
